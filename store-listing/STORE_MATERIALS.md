### Full Description — Google Play

**StreamFlow: Global Radio & Music Chat**

StreamFlow brings radio stations from every corner of the world to your fingertips. Whether you're looking for jazz from Tokyo, electronic beats from Berlin, or classical music from Vienna, StreamFlow makes it simple to explore and enjoy.

**What you can do:**

• Listen to thousands of radio stations organized by genre, mood, and era
• Discover new music through curated collections
• Chat with other listeners in real time while enjoying the same station
• Share your favorite stations with friends
• Customize your listening experience with themes and visual effects
• Save your favorite stations for quick access

**A calm listening experience**

StreamFlow is designed to feel spacious and unhurried. Browse genres like Jazz, Electronic, Classical, and Hip-Hop, or explore moods like Chill, Energetic, and Atmospheric. The interface stays out of your way so you can focus on the music.

**Connect with listeners worldwide**

While you listen, you can join conversations with people who share your taste in music. All chats are private and encrypted, creating a safe space to discuss what you're hearing.

**No subscriptions. No ads interrupting your music.**

StreamFlow is built for people who love radio and want a simple, beautiful way to explore it.

StreamFlow is designed to feel open and unhurried. No endless scrolling, no algorithmic pressure. Just radio, and the people who love it.

## PART 3: ICON & ASSETS

### App Icon Concept

**Visual Direction:**

Create a minimal, atmospheric icon that suggests both **flow** and **radio waves**.

**Core Elements:**
- **Primary shape:** Concentric circles or sound waves emanating from a center point
- **Style:** Gradient, soft glow, ethereal
- **Mood:** Calm, modern, slightly cosmic
- **Color palette:** Deep purple to cyan gradient (matching app theme: `#bc6ff1` → `#00d2ff`)

**What to avoid:**
- No text or letters
- No realistic radio equipment
- No overly complex shapes
- No sharp edges — keep it organic and flowing

**Concept:**
A glowing orb or sphere with subtle radio wave rings expanding outward, set against a dark gradient background. The waves should feel gentle and rhythmic, like music itself.

---

### Icon Generation Prompt

```
A minimal app icon for a music streaming app called StreamFlow. 
Central glowing sphere in vibrant purple (#bc6ff1) with soft cyan accents (#00d2ff). 
Concentric circular waves radiating outward, suggesting sound and radio transmission. 
Deep space background transitioning from dark navy to black. 
Smooth gradients, ethereal glow effects, modern and calm aesthetic. 
No text, no letters, no realistic objects. 
Geometric and abstract, suitable for both iOS and Android. 
Square format, flat design with subtle depth.
```

---

### Required Icon Sizes

**Android (Google Play):**
- 512 x 512 px (uploaded to Play Console, high-res PNG)
- Adaptive icon layers (if using):
  - Foreground: 108 x 108 dp safe zone
  - Background: 108 x 108 dp

**iOS (Apple App Store):**
- 1024 x 1024 px (App Store listing)
- Additional sizes for Xcode asset catalog:
  - 20x20, 29x29, 40x40, 60x60, 76x76, 83.5x83.5 (all @1x, @2x, @3x as needed)

**Recommendation:**
Generate the icon at **2048 x 2048 px** and scale down as needed for perfect quality.

### Policy Compliance Notes

**Google Play Policies:**
- ✓ App must not impersonate other apps
- ✓ User-generated content must be moderated (you have moderation system)
- ✓ Must disclose data collection in Data Safety form
- ✓ Chat features require proper moderation and reporting mechanisms
- ⚠ If using WebView (TWA), ensure 90%+ of content is owned or licensed

**Apple App Store Policies:**
- ✓ Apps using WebView must provide unique value beyond mobile website
- ✓ Must justify native wrapper (background audio, offline, push notifications)
- ✓ User-generated content requires reporting/blocking features
- ✓ End-to-end encryption must be declared in export compliance
- ⚠ App may face extra scrutiny if primarily WebView-based
  - Be prepared to explain offline features, native integrations

**Critical: Privacy Policy Must Include:**
- What data is collected (location, usage stats, chat messages)
- How chat encryption works (E2EE, we don't see messages)
- Whether analytics/tracking is used
- How users can request data deletion
- Contact information for privacy inquiries
